Studied convergence of heat equation solutions towards a Gaussian self-similar profile as t → ∞, deriving optimal convergence rates of order t⁻¹/² and t⁻¹ under finite moment conditions. Supervised by Prof. Nikita Simonov (Sorbonne – LJLL).
