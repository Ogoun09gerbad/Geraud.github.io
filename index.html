Featured Work

Master's Thesis Functional Analysis IMSP · Benin · 2025

Asymptotic Behavior & Self-Similar Profiles of Solutions
to the Heat Equation in ℝⁿ

Using the Fourier transform and semigroup theory, this thesis establishes that every integrable solution to the heat Cauchy problem converges as t → ∞ toward a Gaussian self-similar profile of equal mass — a PDE analogue of the Central Limit Theorem. Convergence rates of order t⁻¹/² and t⁻¹ are derived under finite first- and second-moment conditions, and proved optimal via explicit counterexamples.
